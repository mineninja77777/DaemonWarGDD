Overview

Setting

Setting is medieval, but you are in a facility where superwarriors are created. There are 5, including you. Barbarian, Archer, Rogue, Mage, Beast Handler. You must help win a war that your side has been losing.

Classes

  • Barbarian - Strong and tanky
  • Archer - long range damage
  • Rogue - fast and sneaky
  • Mage - versatile
  • Beast Handler - can tame beasts and influence enemies

Main Mechanics

You level up as you beat battles.
Every levelup you gain a class feature and can increase a stat by 1.
Stats are STR DEX CON ARC.
