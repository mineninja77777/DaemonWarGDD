Basic Enemies

Gargoyle

The gargoyle is usually stone(cannot be damaged while stone) but comes to life when something is within TBC distance of it. When it goes to half health it becomes stone again for 30s.
Damage - 2 + 4*CR
Attack Cooldown - 3s
Health - 10 + 5*CR

Imp

Imps are small and fast, essentially baby daemons
Damage - 1*CR
Attack Cooldown - 0.5s
Health - 2 + 2*CR

Arcane Daemon

Can cast spells
Health - 2*CR + 5
Spell #1 - deathly aura
summons an aura that is TBC large. Any Allies that enter the aura takes 1+CR damage per second. Any Daemons that enter the aura heal the same amount per second
Cooldown 60s
Spell #2 - Flurry of Shards
Sends 20 ice shards out over an area quite quickly. anything hit takes 1.5*CR damage
cooldown 10s
Spell #3 - Army of Beasts
Summons 5 Imps
cooldown 60s

Daegloth

Very buff
Health - 8*CR + 8
Punch
Damage - 6*CR + 2
Cooldown - 2s
Smash (AOE)
Damage - 7*CR + 4
Cooldown - 10s

Shifter

When player comes close, Shifts
1st form - Unshifted - as soon as hit shifts to random 2 - 4
2nd form - Daegloth(weak) - daegloth but looks slightly different and 1/4 Stats
3rd form - Imp(weak) - looks slightly different and 1/4 Stats
4th form - Gargoyle(weak) - looks slightly different and 1/4 Stats
