Intro

The Barbarian is the class with the highest hit points and melee attack damage. It has access to two weapons: the Hammer and the Sword.

Stats

Hammer Damage: 5+(5*level)
Hammer Cooldown: 3s
Sword Damage: 3+(3*level)
