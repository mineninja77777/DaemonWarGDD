Intro

The Rogue is fast and can hide from enemies. It can use a dagger or a rapier.

Stats

Dagger Damage: 2+(2*level)
Dagger Cooldown: 0.5s
Rapier Damage: 3+(3*level)
Rapier Cooldown: 1s
